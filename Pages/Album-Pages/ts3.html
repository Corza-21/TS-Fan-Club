"Speak Now" is Taylor Swift's 3rd album, released on October 25th, 2010. After being hit with accusations from the media that Taylor wasn't pulling her weight in those writing sessions, she decided that she would write her third studio album all by herself, which she did!!
This album features 14 tracks from the standard version of the album + 2 deluxe edition tracks + 6 vault tracks, making it a 22 track album.

This album starts to see Taylor leaning towards the pop space more than before. It is still considered a country-pop album, but it's always being debated.
