Track 6 on "evermore".

"no body, no crime" is an imaginative take on Swift's part on a true crime story, featuring HAIM band member Este Haim—good friends of Swift's—as the center of a missing persons case after being cheated on by her husband. The track itself is a return to Swift's country roots, and its credits include instrumentals from Josh Kaufman from Bonny Light Horseman and Muzz.
