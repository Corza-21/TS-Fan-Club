Track 17 on "evermore".

In addition to the fifteen tracks released on the standard issue of evermore, two bonus tracks-"it's time to go" and "​r​ight where you left me"-were made available on the physical deluxe edition of the album. The songs were released digitally on January 7, 2021.

On "it's time to go" Swift depicts situations where relationships have turned sour, notably mentioning her highly-publicized legal battle with her former label, Big Machine Records, for the ownership rights to the masters of her first six studio albums.
