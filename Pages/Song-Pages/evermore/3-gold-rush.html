Track 3 on "evermore".

"gold rush", the third song on Taylor Swift's ninth studio album evermore, is a song depicting the jealousy she has because she is attracted to someone who everyone admires.

Because of this, she has to stop picturing her life with them. She is singing about dreaming for someone that everybody else wants. She is daydreaming about someone then snapping out of it. She is catching herself getting the idea of this person stuck in her head.

The narrator's dislike of jealousy and anxiety in relationships, and possible self-esteem issues, leads them to decide their fantasy relationship isn't worth it without giving it a try.
