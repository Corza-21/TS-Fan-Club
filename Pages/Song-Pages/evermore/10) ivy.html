Track 10 on "evermore".

"ivy", the tenth track from evermore, tells the story of a married woman falling in love with a person who is not her husband, leading to an affair.

Swift has sung about infidelity in previous songs, including "illicit affairs" (which is also the 10th track) and "august" from her eighth album, folklore.

Also, "ivy", alongside "tolerate it," and "no body, no crime", is part of the "unhappily ever after" anthology of marriages, which reunites infidelity, pure toleration and even murder.
