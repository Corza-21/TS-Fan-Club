Track 12 on "evermore".

"long story short" is a brisk-paced overview of the public demonizations that Taylor has experienced since her feud with Kanye West in 2016. The drama has taken subject in numerous songs of Taylor's, appearing all the way back on her sixth studio album, reputation. However, "long story short" offers a confirmation of resolution and peace, with Taylor finally feeling confident that that stage of her life has subsided.

"long story short" is a particular ode to Taylor's current boyfriend, Joe Alwyn, who she began dating around the time of the drama. Chronicled in a handful of Taylor's songs, "long story short" further emphasizes that Alwyn has always been her "safe place" throughout turmoil.
