Track 14 on "evermore".

"closure" is the 14th track on evermore. It is a wild industrial-folk number with Nine Inch Nails-style drums.

In her Apple Music Awards 2020 interview, Taylor notes that closure is one of the overarching themes in the album: "Evermore deals a lot in endings of all sorts, shapes, and sizes, all the kinds of ways we can end a relationship, a friendship, something toxic, and the pain that goes along with that."

The track is supposedly about Taylor's former friendship with model Karlie Kloss. Lyrics like "staying friends" and "new life" suggest that they are no longer friends, especially as Taylor and Karlie have no longer been seen together. As she recently married, Karlie's "new life" has spent large amounts of time with her extended Kushner family, Scooter Braun, and new friends.
