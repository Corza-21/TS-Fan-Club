About

From Genius, the ultimate source of music knowledge

Track 11 on "reputation".

"Dancing With Our Hands Tied" shows a different side of Taylor's sound not present in much of her previous music. The song has house music vibes and heavy beat drops. In the last chorus of the song Taylor also hits notes in vocal ranges she has not hit in the history of her music career, the notes F5 and G5.
