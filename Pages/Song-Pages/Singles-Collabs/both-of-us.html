This song is a ✨Single✨.

Strange Clouds' third single was one that shocked fans when it was announced in March 2012 that B.o.B had collaborated with country singer Taylor Swift. The song's uplifting lyrics coupled with Taylor's melodic and catchy hook made the song a success, with it going on to be certified Platinum in the US and Australia.
