This song is a ✨Single✨.

“Babe” is a song Taylor Swift wrote with Pat Monahan of Train while working on her 2012 album, Red. The track, which fans believe is about actor Jake Gyllenhaal, finds Taylor looking back on a failed relationship. It's interesting to note that “Better Man,” a song Taylor gave to Little Big Town in 2017, has very similar themes.
