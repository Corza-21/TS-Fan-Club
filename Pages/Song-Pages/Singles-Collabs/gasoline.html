About

This song is a ✨Single✨.

Off of HAIM's 2020 album Women In Music Pt. III, “Gasoline” is a sexy track about a possibly-dysfunctional relationship.

Said Danielle Haim:

That slowed-down ending is due to Ariel. He had this crazy EDM filter he stuck on the guitar, and I was like, 'Yes, that's fucking perfect.'

The band recorded a live performance of the track for Jimmy Kimmel Live. The video was shot during the summer of the 2020 COVID-19 pandemic in the empty parking lot of the “The Forum”, a music venue in Inglewood, California

A remix featuring Taylor Swift was released on February 18th, 2021, and featured on the extended edition of Women In Music Pt III.
