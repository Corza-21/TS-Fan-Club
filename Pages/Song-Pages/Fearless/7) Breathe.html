Track 7 on "Fearless".

"Breathe (Taylor's Version)" is the seventh track from Fearless (Taylor's Version) and features country artist, Colbie Caillat.

Lyrically, this song finds both artists singing about the loss of a friendship, what can present more difficulties to move on, since once someone that means a lot to you goes away, it gets much tougher to accept it â€” It's believed Swift wrote it for her friend, Emily Poe, who used to be a part of Taylor's band, but left in 2007 to focus on law school.

On April 8th, a snippet of this song's version was released on Tumblr, a day before the album release.
