Track 13 on "Fearless".

"Change (Taylor's Version)" is the thirteenth track from Swift's s first re-recorded album, Fearless (Taylor's Version).

Lyrically, this song finds Swift fighting for a chance for her dreams to come true as a superstar. Since, she was sixteen when she accepted to be part of Big Machine Records, — that was the smallest record label in Nashville — at the time, she was the only artist in the record label with about a dozen employees. However, the bitter and challenging begininng, she prefered to stay and see that the rise would light up their path soon.

Furthermore, Swift has written a few lines of the track, however it was left for a while, since she was wishing for a incredible occasion to get into her life for her to finally be able to finish it.

So, in 2007, when Swift won the Horizon Award at the CMAs, she looked out into all the place and sees her then manager, Scott Borchetta, in tears. That was the moment, she could finally complete the song, which occured in the following day.
