Track 20 on "Fearless".

"Today Was A Fairytale (Taylor's Version)" is the twentieth track of Taylor Swift's first rerecorded album, Fearless (Taylor's Version).

"Today Was a Fairytale" was originally written for the 2010 movie Taylor acted in, Valentine's Day. As a song originally released by Big Machine Records, it was included in the release of Fearless (Taylor's Version) since it was written around the same time as Fearless.
