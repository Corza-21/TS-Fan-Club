From Genius, the ultimate source of music knowledge

Track 11 on "Fearless".

Lyrically, "Forever & Always (Taylor's Version)" finds Swift looking back on an affair which her lover promised to stay, but she just watched him gradually fade away.

This song is assumed to be inspired about her relationship, especially the break up, with american singer Joe Jonas. On 2010, Swift described it as a song about a relationship with a man who she could feel was "slowly slipping away." She went on to add:

It's about the confusion and frustration of wondering why? What changed? When did it change? What did I do wrong?

On the piano version for this song, Swift touches on the rawness of the emotions that the lyrics express.
