Track 21 on "Fearless".

"You All Over Me" is a once-unreleased song that leaked in 2017, written in 2005 for Swift's sophomore album, Fearless. Swift released the song in March 2021 as a single from her Fearless (Taylor's Version) project. The song marks the first time that Swift has worked with Maren Morris on record.

Lyrically, the song contains similar thematic elements to her 2014 song "Clean" and describes the feeling of still being connected to a former lover.
