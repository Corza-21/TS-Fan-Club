Track 12 on "Fearless".

"The Best Day (Taylor's Version)" is the twelfth track from Swift's first re-recorded album, Fearless (Taylor's Version).

Here, Swift sings about her childhood, growth and her family, especially her mother, Andrea Swift, who has always been looking out for her, even if she was wrong, sad and happy. Then Swift addresses how much of herself is mirrored of who her mother is.
