Track 25 on "Fearless".

Formerly one of Taylor Swift's rarest unreleased songs, "Don't You" was added to the tracklist of Swift's re-release of her sophomore album.

It describes Swift running into her ex, and the encounter causing her to remember their past together and feel the pain of their history together that hurt her. She doesn't understand how this person doesn't feel the same pain that she does, but resigns herself to moving on with her life, and rejecting the person's desire to pretend to stay friends.

On April 2nd 2021, Taylor posted a video on her social media where the vault was opened and words with the letters mixed up came out of it. This song was reversed and used as the background music for the video.
