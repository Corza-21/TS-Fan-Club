This song is ✨Unreleased✨.

"Her" is an unreleased song by American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift.[1] This unreleased song seems to be about the same situation as 2006's “Teardrops on My Guitar” and an unreleased track “Permanent Marker”. Much like another unreleased track “Angelina”, Swift describes the “other girl” that stole her crush's heart. It leaked online in October 29, 2021.
