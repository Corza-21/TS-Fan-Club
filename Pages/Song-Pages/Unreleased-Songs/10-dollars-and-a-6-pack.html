“Ten Dollars And A Six Pack” alternatively known as “10 Dollars And A 6 Pack” is a song written and sung by young Taylor swift.

The song is about an attractive country boy who Taylor thought she could never have. Even though everyone tells her that he was 'bad news'. She goes out with him anyway and it turns out everyone was right.
