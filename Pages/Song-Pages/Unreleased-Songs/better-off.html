“Better Off” is an unreleased song by Taylor Swift. The song is rumoured to be an outtake from her self-titled debut studio album, Taylor Swift.

On January 4, 2023, “Better Off” appeared as a recently registered song through ASCAP and Sony, along with “Bother Me”, “Castle's Crumbling” and “Drama Queen”.

In “Better Off”, Taylor describes being in a relationship with a boy her friends don't approve of. They say that he's trouble, and she should find somebody else. In the end, Taylor doesn't listen to them, and finds happiness with the boy she loves.
