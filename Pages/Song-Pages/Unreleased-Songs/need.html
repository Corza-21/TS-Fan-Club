About

From Genius, the ultimate source of music knowledge

Unreleased on "Lover".

"Need" is a reject from Swift's August 2019 album, which the infamous group Taylor's Inner Circle obtained in March 2020. The group describes the song, saying:

The music is unlike any other Taylor song with the chords played in a unique way starting and stopping (sometimes with vocal effects added over). Taylor gives fascinating insight into her thoughts on want, desire, passion, lust, danger, and fear. What Taylor needs most is to be with the man she loves. It's essential, like breathing for her. The music is stripped down during the bridge. A really good song that would have fit well on the album and perhaps been a fan favorite.
