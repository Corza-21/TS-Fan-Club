"Smokey Black Nights" is an unreleased song by Taylor Swift. It is the fifteenth track on "Taylor's Songs".

Taylor started writing "Smokey Black Nights" while on a vacation in Stone Harbor in the summer of 2002. As seen in a diary in her 2020 documentary Miss Americana, she finished a first draft on January 19, 2003. In a 2009 email exchange with The Philadelphia Inquirer about her days on the shore, Taylor spoke about the song:

There was a little demo I made when I was 11, and I think it's floating around the Internet. It's called "Smokey Black Nights" and it's all about being at the shore.
