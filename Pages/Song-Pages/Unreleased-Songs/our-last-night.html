"Our Last Night" is Taylor Swift cover of Better Than Ezra's song "Our Last Night".

This song is about letting go of someone. They wanted different things and they had to part ways.

He had big dreams and had to go away. She just wanted to stay the way they were - It's the opposite (he/she) in the original song.
