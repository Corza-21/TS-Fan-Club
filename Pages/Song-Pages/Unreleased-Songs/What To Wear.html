"What To Wear" is an unreleased Taylor Swift song.

It is an upbeat, sweet song about a girl getting ready to attend her school dance. It details her excitement about dressing up, seeing the boy she likes, and having the night of her dreams.

By the end of the song, the days of school have passed, but the girl carries on with her life in happiness.
