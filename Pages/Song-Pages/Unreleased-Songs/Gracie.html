This song is ✨Unreleased✨.

Swift wrote “Gracie” about a young girl named Gracie Johnson who she met on a flight before releasing her 2006 debut album. Gracie had leukemia and passed away in 2019. Swift included a picture of herself with Gracie in the lyric video for "Love Story (Taylor's Version)" in 2021.
