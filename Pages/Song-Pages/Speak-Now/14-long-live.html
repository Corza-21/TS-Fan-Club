Track 14 on "Speak Now".

"Long Live" is the last track on the standard version of Taylor's 2010 album, Speak Now. In the song, Taylor finds herself looking back at her fondest memories of her career, none of which would be possible without her fanbase and band, who the song is dedicated to.

The secret message for this song in the CD's lyric booklet is "For You."
