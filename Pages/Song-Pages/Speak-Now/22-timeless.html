Track 22 on "Speak Now".

In the nostalgic “Timeless,” Taylor narrates the story of an age old couple from the girl's point of view. Presumed to be about her grandparents, Marjorie and Dean, Taylor emphasizes on the passion and certainty of fate surrounding their relationship.

Reminiscent of her classic country songs, including “Untouchable (Taylor's Version)” and “I Bet You Think About Me (Taylor's Version),” the mixture of piano, drums and guitar creates a sentimental and historic tone to the song. In an effort to detail the inevitable occurrence of her grandparents' love story, the rhythm and tempo of the music accelerates each time in the chorus.

This notes the second time Taylor mentioning and detailing parts of her grandparents' lives. She first introduced her grandmother on evermore's “marjorie,” while tributing her grandfather on “epiphany.” It's interesting to note the lyric video showing several photos and videos of her grandparents, either together or on their own.
