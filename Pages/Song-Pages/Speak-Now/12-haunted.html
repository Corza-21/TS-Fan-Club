About

From Genius, the ultimate source of music knowledge

Track 12 on "Speak Now".

In "Haunted," Taylor writes about a past relationship in this song which the memory of will not escape her mind. The boy and the relationship will haunt her for the rest of her life.

On the Speak Now World Tour, Taylor revved up the drama for the song by starting it with bells and wearing a dark, ripped-up dress.
