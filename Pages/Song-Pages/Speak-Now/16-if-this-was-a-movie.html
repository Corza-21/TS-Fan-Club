About

From Genius, the ultimate source of music knowledge

Track 16 on "Speak Now".

In "If This Was A Movie," Taylor daydreams about a lost love coming back to her.

The track is only available on the deluxe edition of Taylor's 2010 album Speak Now. Lyrically and thematically, it resembles her 2008 song "Breathe."

The track has received some backlash for supposedly being grammatically incorrect.

People say that it should be "If This Were A Movie" rather than "If This Was A Movie".

It is the only song on the album with a co-writer.
