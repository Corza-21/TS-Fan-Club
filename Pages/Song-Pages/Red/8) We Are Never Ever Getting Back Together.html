Track 8 on "Red".

"We Are Never Ever Getting Back Together (Taylor's Version)" is the 8th track off of Taylor's 2nd re-recorded album, "Red (Taylor's Version)."

Taylor Swift refuses to continue an on-and-off relationship with her boyfriend and declares that "We Are Never Ever Getting Back Together".

Originally, the song was released as the lead single for Taylor Swift's fourth studio album, Red.
