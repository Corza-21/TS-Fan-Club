Track 13 on "Red".

"The Lucky One (Taylor's Version)" is the thirteenth track from Swift's second re-recorded album, Red (Taylor's Version).

This song tells the story of a star who had lots of achievements and disappointments in her life, since she suffered a wide exposure of her secrets to the public. After these episodes, this star decided to live a more peaceful life, which one wouldn't be on the spotlight.

By the end of record, Swift leaves her understanding why this star moved away, since now she's “The Lucky One”.
