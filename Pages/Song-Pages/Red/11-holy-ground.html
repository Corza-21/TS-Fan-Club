Track 11 on "Red".

"Holy Ground (Taylor's Version)" is the eleventh track from Swift's second re-recorded album Red (Taylor's Version).

Lyrically, this song finds Swift looking back on a past relationship that didn't last and receive the best ending, but realizing, even if short-lived, it was good for the memories and learnings they have shared.

This song's subject is speculated to be Joe Jonas, since the hidden message for this track was "When you came to the show in SD" which occuring was during Swift's Speak Now Tour and Joe Jonas was spotted watching Swift performing in San Diego.
