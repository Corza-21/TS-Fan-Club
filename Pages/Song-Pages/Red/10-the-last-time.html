About

From Genius, the ultimate source of music knowledge

Track 10 on "Red".

Lyrically, "The Last Time" tells the story of a couple on which the girl is fed up with her lover's promises about them. From the other side, the boy tries to convince her to come back together with him.

This song is speculated to be about actor Jake Gyllenhaal, since this song's concept and meaning is similar to "We Are Never Ever Getting Back Together" and "I Almost Do", which are other Red tracks assumed to be about the actor.
