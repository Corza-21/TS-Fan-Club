Track 16 on "Midnights".

"Paris" is the sixteenth track on Taylor Swift's tenth album, Midnights. It was released as apart of the surprise drop of Midnights 3AM edition and is the third Taylor swift song to have a city in the title - the others being "London Boy" and "Welcome To New York".

Lyrically the song discusses being in love with someone and ignoring or blocking out other distractions, like gossip and paparazzi, because of being consumed with and transported by life with this person and making imagined adventures out of simple things with them.
