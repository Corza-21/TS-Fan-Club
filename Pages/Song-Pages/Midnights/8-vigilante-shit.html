Track 8 on "Midnights".

"Vigilante Shit" follows the topic of a woman who's seeking revenge. Swift and the subject of the song ostensibly obtain their revenge - instead of getting sad, they get even.

The themes of this song heavily parallels Swift 2020's track "no body, no crime", which also sees her involved in vigilantism.

Musically, this song continues with heavy synths reverberating, never quite building to its climax. The sultry, building tune creates a dreamy atmosphere similar to that of "Dress" or "Call It What You Want", but the subject matter is considerably darker.
