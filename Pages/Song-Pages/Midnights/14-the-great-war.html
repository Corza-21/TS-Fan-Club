Track 14 on "Midnights".

"The Great War" is a track on the 3am Edition of Midnights. It details a devastating conflict within a romantic relationship that is instigated when one partner suspects the other of infidelity, using battle and funerary imagery to demonstrate its impact.

Despite the conflict, "The Great War" delivers a hopeful message, describing the couple emerging from the tension together and promising to love one another "always" since they have already survived a a difficult period together.
