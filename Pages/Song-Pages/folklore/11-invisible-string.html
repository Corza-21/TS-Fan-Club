Track 11 on "folklore".

"invisible string" references an east Asian folk myth about a red thread of fate tying two soulmates together. Like on Swift's 2019 track "Lover," she expresses gratitude for past romantic failures since they paved the way for her relationship with Joe Alwyn.

The song includes several nods to Swift's past work, including October 2014's "Bad Blood" and November 2017's "Delicate." Like on October 2012's "Red" and August 2019's "Daylight," she uses the connotations of different colors to convey the intensity of her love for a partner.
