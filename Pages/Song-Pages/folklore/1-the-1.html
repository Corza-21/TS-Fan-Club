Track 1 on "folklore".

"the 1," the second single from the album, centers around the nostalgic remembrance of myths and lost loves. Taylor contemplates an alternate life in which her old wishes had "come true" and resulted in a relationship with an ex-lover.

Sharing a similar sentiment to Swift's track "I Almost Do" from October 2012's Red, "the 1" is a callback to the folk moments from the same record, folk music being a defining influence on folklore.
