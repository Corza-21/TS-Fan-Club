About

From Genius, the ultimate source of music knowledge

Track 8 on "Lover".

Swift reveals her commitment to her lover, recounting the story of their love and declaring that she is so willing to stay with him forever, she would forgo any formalities and marry him with homemade paper rings.

The song is likely about her current boyfriend of 7+ years, Joe Alwyn.

Swift has shared the concept of this song at the secret sessions with some fans:

The whole song is just basically reminiscing on fun memories. And then in the chorus, it talks about how [â€¦], your whole life you talk with your friends about how, like, 'Oh my God. Do you wanna get married? What do you want your ring to look like? What kind of ring do you want?' I don't know, I just feel like if you really love someone, love someone, you'd be like, 'I don't care.' And so, it talks about that concept as the hook.
