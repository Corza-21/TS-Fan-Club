Track 14 on "Taylor Swift".

"A Perfectly Good Heart" is one of Taylor Swift's first breakup songs. In it, she describes her very first experience with heartbreak, yet also hints there are more to come in her future.

It appears as the fourteenth track on her eponymous debut, and is only available on the deluxe edition.
