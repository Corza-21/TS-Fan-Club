About

From Genius, the ultimate source of music knowledge

This song is a ✨Single✨.

Three of country's brightest stars team up for a song about both relationships and road safety. Tim McGraw tells a story in which a not-so-great driver is distracted, and turns on the radio, which happens to be playing a nonexistent Taylor Swift song, to tune out her thoughts. Keith Urban plays a guitar solo towards the end of the track.

Funnily, Taylor's first big hit was called "Tim McGraw."
