This song is a ✨Single✨.

“Two Is Better Than One” is the second single off the Boys Like Girls' album Love Drunk and features pop starlet, Taylor Swift, who also co-wrote the song. The pair also collaborated to pen the track “You'll Always Find Your Way Back Home” for the soundtrack of Hannah Montana: The Movie.

This song performed well on charts, even reaching number 18 on the Canadian Billboard Hot 100.

The song laments on the singer's realization that he needs a particular person in his life despite his previous doubts. This reflective, sombre piece is a love story at heart like many of the other tracks on Love Drunk, but takes a more vulnerable and delicate approach than many of the band's more uptempo, rock songs.

Interestingly, Taylor and Martin are also rumored to have had a brief relationship at the time the song was written.
