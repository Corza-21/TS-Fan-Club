Track 6 on "The More Red (Taylor's Version) Chapter - EP".

Taylor Swift announced on March 16th that as a surprise, she would release 4 songs at midnight to celebrate the first night of The Eras Tour. One of those songs being Safe & Sound (Taylor's Version).

Safe & Sound was a duet she did The Civil Wars in 2012 for the soundtrack of the first "The Hunger Games" Movie.

"Safe & Sound" is a lullaby sung by Katniss' character to her 12-year-old sister Prim. In the song, Katniss promises to protect her and keep them both safe, even though she knows she probably can't. The haunting song was written by Swift and features Joy Williams And John Paul White with their signature intricate harmonies.
