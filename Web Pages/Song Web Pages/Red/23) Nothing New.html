About

From Genius, the ultimate source of music knowledge

Track 23 on "Red".

Swift mentions "Nothing New" in the journals she released alongside her 2019 record Lover. In the journal, she reveals she wrote it for her 2012 record Red — she later included it on 2021's Red (Taylor's Version) as a "From the Vault" track.

The track was inspired by Joni Mitchell's "A Case Of You."
