Track 22 on "Red".

A cut from the original 2012 Red album, "Better Man" captures the feeling of getting out of a toxic, perhaps abusive, relationship and accepting the sorrow that follows the breakup. Swift gave the song to Little Big Town in 2016, making it her first country song since she crossed over to pop with her 2014 record 1989.

Little Big Town's rendition of the song won a Grammy for "Best Country Duo/Group Performance" at the 2018 Grammy Awards.
