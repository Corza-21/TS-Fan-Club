Track 3 on "Red".

"Treacherous (Taylor's Version)" is the third track on Swift's second re-recorded album, Red (Taylor's version).

Treacherous is also a word to define something harmful and that possibly presents some deceit.

Lyrically, this song finds Swift singing about a relationship as metaphor for an affair that is occurring pretty fast and might not be the best and healthiest path for her to get into, however she cares to watch it until its end. Throughout the song, the lyrics contain a pretty strong sensual references which could direct metaphors for the act.

There are thousand of theories that speculate who is the subject of this song, being John Mayer and Jake Gyllenhaal assumed in the conversation, nevertheless, the hints point out Harry Styles, since, at the time, Swift stated to USA Today that "Treacherous" and "I Knew You Were Trouble" are about the same person, and "I Knew You Were Trouble" is another song possibly about Harry Styles.

Furthermore, both "Treacherous" and "I Knew You Were Trouble" present similarities on the concept of recognizing someone or something is not good for you. Even though "Treacherous" tells the beginning of the affair, and "I Knew You Were Trouble" focuses on the breakup and after.
