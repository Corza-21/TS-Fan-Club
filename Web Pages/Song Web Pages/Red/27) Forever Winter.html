Track 27 on "Red".

On this passionate winter ballad dubbed "Forever Winter", Taylor sings of her constant worry and stress over the actions of her friend. Noting the miscommunication in their relationship, and speaking of the disrupted mental health that they endure day by day.

This track is rumoured to be about an old high school friend named Jeff Lang, who was struggling with addiction and mental health issues in the past.
