“4 U” alternatively known as “For You” is an unreleased Taylor Swift song from “Taylor Swift Volume 2” - her second demo CD.

In this song, Taylor thanks an unknown person, perhaps a lover, friend, or mentor, for coming into her life and changing it for the better, even if they are too humble to accept her praises.
