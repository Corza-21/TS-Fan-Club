"Angelina" is an unreleased song by Taylor Swift.

This song tells a story about a girl who is dating Taylor's crush. It is possible that the crush is Stephen Barker Liles of Love and Theft — Taylor wrote "Hey Stephen" about him, and it landed on her 2008 album Fearless.
