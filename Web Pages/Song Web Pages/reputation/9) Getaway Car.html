About

From Genius, the ultimate source of music knowledge

Track 9 on "reputation".

Taylor uses the image of criminals in a speeding car as the central metaphor for a doomed romance. She likens her rebound relationship with Tom Hiddleston to a "getaway car" ride used to get away from a crime scene, or her failed relationship with Calvin Harris.

Like on her 2014 track "Wonderland," she includes a pop-culture reference to connect her experiences to something that listeners are familiar with, adding another layer of livelihood to the story.

Co-writer Jack Antonoff, also known by his stage name Bleachers, has released a song called "Rollercoaster" that contains similar melodies and another shotgun metaphor.
