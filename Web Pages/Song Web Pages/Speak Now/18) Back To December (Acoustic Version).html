About

Track 18 on "Speak Now".

"Back To December (Acoustic Version)" is a more cut back version of track 3 on Speak Now, Back To December.

In this version of the song, Taylor's sings in a way as if more reflective than the oringinal version, but the vocals do seem to be re-used.
