Track 17 on "Speak Now".

"Superman" is a track on the deluxe edition of Taylor Swift's 2010 album Speak Now. In the song, Taylor describes a boy who left her and hopes he will eventually come back to her when the time is right.

Taylor has been quoted to say the following about the inspiration of the track:

Taylor was inspired to write Superman when, after the guy it was about walked out of the room, she turned and said to a friend, "Hey, it's just like watching superman fly away."

The song is rumored to be about John Mayer, whom Taylor briefly dated in 2010.
