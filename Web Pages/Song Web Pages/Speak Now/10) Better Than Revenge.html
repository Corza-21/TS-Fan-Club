About

From Genius, the ultimate source of music knowledge

Track 10 on "Speak Now".

"Better Than Revenge" is rumored to be about actress Camilla Belle, who infamously stole Joe Jonas' heart while he was dating Taylor.

Further evidence proves that this song is about Camilla Belle, if you take into consideration one of the last lines of the song, where she says the words "much better," which is also the name of a Jonas Brothers' song.
