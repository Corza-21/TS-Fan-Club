Track 15 on "Fearless".

"Untouchable (Taylor's Version)"is the fifteenth track from Swift's first re-recorded album, Fearless (Taylor's Version).

Lyrically, the song finds Swift wishing for her lover to come to me for them to be together, however, for some reason, they are not succeeding.

Swift also covered, changed a few lyrics and turned the rock song of the same name from a 1999 rock band, Luna Halo, into a slow dreamy country ballad.
