Track 1 on "Fearless".

A snippet of "Fearless (Taylor's Version)" was released on April 8th, on Good Morning America. According to Swift herself, this track is lyrically about "the fearlessness of falling in love, and no matter how many break up songs you write, no matter how many times you get hurt, you will always fall in love again".

Following the release of the first version of Fearless, Swift also explained what the title meant to her on the album booklet:

To me, 'fearless' is not the absence of fear. It's not being completely unafraid. To me, fearless is having fears. Fearless is having doubts. Lots of them. To me, fearless is living in spite of those things that scare you to death. Fearless is falling madly in love again, even though you've been hurt before. [â€¦] No matter what love throws at you, you have to believe in it. You have to believe in love stories and Prince Charmings and happily ever after. That's why I write these songs. Because I think love is fearless.
