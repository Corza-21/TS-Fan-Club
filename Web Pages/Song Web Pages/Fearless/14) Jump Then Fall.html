Track 14 on "Fearless".

"Jump Then Fall (Taylor's Version)" is the fourteenth track from Swift's first-re-recorded album, Fearless (Taylors's Version).

Lyrically, the song finds Swift telling a summer lover that no matter the difficulty they might eventually face, she will be there with him, as she wants him to be with her.

Swift explained the track to MTV News back in 2009, describing it as bouncy, happy, lovely and a feel-good song.

Interesting, this is Swift's only song that start with the letter "J".
