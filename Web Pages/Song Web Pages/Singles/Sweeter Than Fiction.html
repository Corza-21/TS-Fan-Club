"Sweeter Than Fiction" is an empowering song from the soundtrack for the British feel good film, One Chance, about Paul Potts, a singer who won Britain's Got Talent in 2007. It was the lead single from the soundtrack, and was released on October 21st, 2013. Little did fans know, this was a hint at the pop direction that Taylor would embark in for her fifth studio album.

This song was Taylor's first time working with Jack Antonoff, who is part of both Fun. and Bleachers. The two went on to collaborate on Taylor's 2014 album 1989, her 2017 album reputation., her 2019 album Lover, her 2020 albums folklore and evermore, and her 2021 re-recording Fearless (Taylor's Version) and Red (Taylor's Version).
