About

From Genius, the ultimate source of music knowledge

Track 10 on "1989".

Taylor Swift unveiled a little snippet of this song in her Diet Coke ad.

The song works as an advice to a guy who wants to get a girl back. She suggests better tactics than the ones her former lovers seemed to have used on her, described in songs like "We Are Never Ever Getting Back Together".

The song has also been rumored to be about the on/off relationship between Justin Bieber and Selena Gomez.
