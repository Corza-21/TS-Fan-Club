Track 23 on "Midnights".

“Karma (Remix)” is a remix of Taylor Swift's 2022 hit, Karma, featuring rapper Ice Spice.

After rumors went out, Swift took to Twitter to officially announce the collaboration alongside the Midnights (Til Dawn Edition), featuring an extended version of “Snow On The Beach” with a new verse from Lana Del Rey.
