Track 7 on "folklore".

This song is about one of Taylor's childhood friends who seemed to have had an unhappy life at home. Taylor reflects back on her innocence back then, how she thought that their problems could be easily solved.

This is reminiscent of the song "It's Nice to Have A Friend", from August 2019's Lover album, which talks about a childhood friend that Taylor grows up with and eventually marries.

On August 20, 2020, Taylor included "seven" on the folklore: the escapism chapter EP, indicating that it fits thematically with folklore's other songs about seeking relief from unpleasantness.
