"Fearless (Taylor's Version)" is a re-recorded version of Taylor Swift's second studio album, "Fearless," released in 2008. The album features all 19 original tracks, along with six previously unreleased songs that were "From the Vault." The lead single, "Love Story (Taylor's Version)," received critical acclaim and became a commercial success, reaching the top 10 on the Billboard Hot 100. The album also includes the bonus track "Today Was a Fairytale," which was previously released as a single in 2010. The updated production and vocal performances on the album showcase Swift's growth as an artist over the past decade and serve as a testament to her resilience and creativity as a musician. Overall, "Fearless (Taylor's Version)" is a must-listen for any fan of Swift's music and a powerful statement of her artistic autonomy and control over her own music.

"Fearless" is Taylor Swift's sophomore album, released on November 11th, 2008. This album was written in sessions with Liz Rose and Hillary Lindsey, however Taylor wrote most of the songs by herself. The album featured 19 tracks, however "Fearless (Taylor's Version)" features those original 19 tracks + "Today Was A Fairytale" + 6 Vault Tracks - all re-recorded to perfection and released April 9th, 2021.

"Fearless" went on to win Album Of The Year at the 2010 Grammys. This album can be considered as a country album, but does have a few notable elements of pop. This album was given 6 music videos, however with the release of Taylor's Version, we weren't given any new music videos.
