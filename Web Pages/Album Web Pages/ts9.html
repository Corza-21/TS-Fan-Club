"evermore" is Taylor Swift's ninth studio album, released on December 11, 2020, just five months after her surprise album "folklore". The album features collaborations with Justin Vernon of Bon Iver, Aaron Dessner of The National, and Jack Antonoff, among others. It was recorded during the same sessions as "folklore" and explores similar themes of love, loss, and longing, but with a darker, more experimental sound.

"evermore" received critical acclaim upon release, with many critics praising the album's cohesive sound and introspective lyrics. The album debuted at number one on the Billboard 200 chart, giving Swift her eighth consecutive number-one debut, and topped charts in several other countries as well.

The album features a number of standout tracks, including "willow," the lead single that debuted at number one on the Billboard Hot 100, "champagne problems," a heartbreaking ballad about a failed engagement, and "evermore," a haunting duet with Justin Vernon.
