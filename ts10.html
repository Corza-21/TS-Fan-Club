Midnights is the tenth studio album by American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift, released on October 21, 2022. It is a concept album about nocturnal ruminations, exploring topics of anxiety, insecurity, self-criticism, self-awareness, insomnia, and self-confidence. The album has 13 tracks on the standard edition and seven bonus tracks on the 3 am edition. It features a collaboration with Lana Del Rey on the song "Snow On The Beach". The album was announced at the 2022 MTV Video Music Awards and the tracklist was revealed on TikTok. The album received critical acclaim for its restrained production, candid songwriting and vocal cadences. It also broke several records for streaming and sales, becoming the best-selling album of 2022. The lead single "Anti-Hero" topped the charts in 15 countries, including the US.

"Midnights" is Taylor Swift's 10th album, released October 21st, 2022. This is an album filled with songs written at Midnight all throughout Taylor's life. "Midnights" was released at Midnight and the first thing you hear when you play the album is 'Meet me at midnight'. "Midnights" is first and foremost a concept album, and this album has already 4 Music Videos to go with it, exploring concepts of depression, anxiety, insecurity, and self-confidence.

"Midnights" features 13 songs (Taylor's lucky number), with the 3am edition of the album featuring 7 additional songs, released just 3 hours after the standard album. The album was also sold at Target with an additional exclusive song "Hits Different" and 2 remixes. Later, this album was released again under a different name of "Midnights (The Til Dawn Edition) which included "Hits Different" and 2 new songs. This was released at the same time as the "Midnights (The Late Night Edition)" was released which included another vault song called "You're Losing Me". This version of the album was released on CD to those going to the East Rutherford shows of The Eras Tour, and was released as a digital 24 hour exclusive deal on Taylor's website.

The album instantly debuted at number 1 on the Billboard Hot 100 and Taylor took up all 10 spots on the Billboard Hot 100, broke streaming records on Spotify and Apple Music in the first day Spotify even went down for a little bit in the first minutes because too many people tried to access the same album at once.
