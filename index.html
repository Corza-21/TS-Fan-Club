Miss Americana

Miss Americana is a documentary that follows Taylor Swift over the course of several years of her career.
Released on the 31st of January 2020, released less than 6 months after she released her album "Lover".
It is named after track 7 on Lover: Miss Americana & The Heartbreak Prince.
It has been described as an "unvarnished and emotionally revealing look into Taylor Swift's life", it's shows a time where she starts to harness the power of her voice.
Along with this documentary, Taylor Swift also released a political song called "Only The Young" for this movie.

Timeline

Taylor Swift (Debut)

Taylor Swift's 1st album has her fully embracing country music and bringing us 15 gorgeous tracks. We were given 5 music videos for this era. This album is yet to be re-recorded.

October 24th 2006
Fearless

Taylor Swift's 2nd album sees her immediately maturing, bringing us 13 emotional tracks, with 6 bonus tracks. We were given 7 music videos for this era. This album was re-recorded in 2021.

November 11th 2008
Fearless Tour

Taylor's "Fearless Tour" was her first worldwide tour, visiting North America, Europe, Australia & Asia. This tour was in support of her second album "Fearless". This tour covered 118 shows.

April 23rd 2009 - July 10th 2010
Speak Now

Taylor Swift's 3rd album which was originally going to be called "Enchanted" is a 14 track album, with 5 bonus tracks. We were given 6 music videos for this era. This album is yet to be re-recorded but most speculate it will be next.

October 25th 2010
Speak Now World Tour

Taylor's "Speak Now World Tour" was her second worldwide tour, visiting North America, Europe, Oceania & Asia. This tour was in support of her third album "Speak Now". This tour covered 110 shows.

February 9th 2011 - March 18th 2012
Red

Taylor Swift's 4th album which was apparently "nOt sOnIcAlLy cOhEsIvE" is a 16 track album, with 4 bonus tracks. We were given 7 music videos for this era. This album was re-recorded in 2021.

October 22nd 2012
Red Tour

Taylor's "Red Tour" was her third worldwide tour, visiting North America, Europe, Oceania & Asia. This tour was in support of her fourth album "Red". This tour covered 86 shows.

March 13th 2013 - June 12th 2014
1989

Taylor Swift's 5th album which was her first full-pop album is a 13 track album, with 3 bonus tracks. We were given 7 music videos for this era. This album is yet to be re-recorded but we have 2 re-recorded singles off this album.

October 27th 2014
1989 World Tour

Taylor's "1989 World Tour" was her fourth worldwide tour, visiting North America, Europe, Oceania & Asia. This tour was in support of her fifth album "1989". This tour covered 85 shows.

May 5th 2015 - December 12th 2015
reputation

Taylor Swift's 6th album, which came after a longer break than ever before is a 15 track album. We were given 4 music videos for this era. This album is yet to be re-recorded.

November 10th 2017
Reputation Stadium Tour

Taylor's "Reputation Stadium Tour" was her fifth worldwide tour, visiting North America, Europe, Oceania & Asia. This tour was in support of her sixth album "reputation". This tour covered 53 shows.

May 8th 2018 - November 21st 2018
Lover

Taylor Swift's 7th album which Taylor was spotted recording during the Reputation Stadium Tour, brings us 18 beautiful tracks. We were given 4 music videos for this era. This album won't be re-recorded but there are unreleased vault tracks from album.

August 23rd 2019
folklore

Taylor Swift's 8th album which came in the midst of a global pandemic includes 16 tracks and 1 bonus track. We were only given 1 music video for this era. This album won't be re-recorded but there are whispers about a cardigan (10 minute version).

July 24th 2020
evermore

Taylor Swift's 9th album which came just 5 months after "folklore" brings 15 intricately crafted tracks, with 2 bonus tracks. We were only given 1 music video for this era. This album won't be re-recorded.

December 11th 2020
Fearless (Taylor's Version)

Taylor Swift's highly anticipated re-recording of "Fearless" features the original 19 tracks + "Today Was A Fairytale" + 6 vault tracks. We didn't get any music videos for this re-recording.

April 9th 2021
Red (Taylor's Version)

Taylor Swift's very highly awaited re-recording of "Red" features the original 20 tracks + "Ronan" + 9 vault tracks. We were given 1 video and 1 short film for this re-recording.

November 12th 2021
Midnights

Taylor Swift's 10th album is a concept album with 13 tracks, 7 "3am tracks", and "Hits Different". So far, we have been given 3 music videos for this era.

October 21st 2022
Eras Tour

The Eras Tour was immediately iconic from the moment it was announced. This tour sold out so fast, the process of buying tickets was made a nightmare by Ticketmaster. This tour still has international dates to be announced.
